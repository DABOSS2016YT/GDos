Terms of Service & Privacy

What we collect

App version and OS platform are sent once per launch so we can understand which versions are in use. Your IP address is immediately hashed with a secret daily salt and discarded — the raw IP is never written anywhere and the hash cannot be reversed or linked across days.

Error reports

If G-Dos crashes, the error message and stack trace may be sent. File paths and personal data are stripped. This helps us fix bugs.

What we do NOT collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, file contents, keystrokes, location data, or any personally identifiable information.

Data retention & sharing

Anonymous counts are kept for up to 12 months. Error reports are capped at 30 entries. Data is stored on our private server and is never sold or shared.

Your rights (GDPR)

You may withdraw consent at any time via Help → Privacy Settings. Because no personally identifiable data is stored there is nothing to retrieve or delete. You may lodge a complaint with the ICO (UK) or your national data protection authority.

Use of the software

G-Dos is provided as-is. We make no warranty of fitness for a particular purpose. By using G-Dos you agree not to use it for unlawful purposes.
